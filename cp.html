Common Questions

Everything you'd want to know before hiring a painter — answered honestly and directly.

Yes — Cascade Valley Painting is fully licensed, bonded, and insured. That means you're protected, your home is protected, and you can have complete peace of mind throughout the entire project. We're happy to provide proof of insurance upon request.
Every project is unique — costs depend on the size of your home, the condition of existing surfaces, and the scope of work. We provide free, no-obligation estimates with clear, itemized pricing so you know exactly what to expect before anything starts. No surprises.
Most interior projects are completed in 2–5 days and exterior jobs typically take 3–7 days, depending on size, weather, and complexity. We'll give you a clear timeline before we start — and we stick to it.
We keep a close eye on the weather and plan accordingly. If rain is expected, we'll adjust the schedule to protect your home and ensure the paint adheres properly. We never apply exterior paint in conditions that would compromise the finish — your investment is too important to rush.
We use drop cloths, plastic sheeting, and painter's tape to carefully protect your floors, furniture, fixtures, and trim before any work begins. We treat your home with the same care we'd want for our own — everything is covered, and everything gets cleaned up before we leave.
We use high-quality, low-VOC paints whenever possible, which significantly reduces odor. For interior projects, we recommend opening windows for ventilation during and after painting. The smell typically fades within a day or two. We're happy to discuss paint options if odor sensitivity is a concern for your household.
